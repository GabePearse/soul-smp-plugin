## Player Commands

### `/team create <name>`
Creates a new team.

### `/team invite <player>`
Invites a player to your team.

### `/team accept`
Accepts the latest invite.

### `/team leave`
Leaves current team.

### `/team disband`
Deletes team.

### `/team banner remove`
Removes team banner anchor.

### `/team info`
Shows team stats and data.

### `/team border`
Visualizes territory for a few seconds.

---

## Chat Commands

### `/tchat`
Toggle team chat.

### `/tmsg <message>`
Send one-off team message.

---

## Admin Commands

### `/soulsmp reload`
Reloads plugin configs.

---
